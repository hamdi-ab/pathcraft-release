# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

## 1. Information We Collect

Pathcraft collects only the information necessary to provide and improve the App's services:

- **Email address**: Collected only if you choose to provide it (e.g., for account-related communications)
- **Usage data**: Information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and session duration
- **Progress data**: Your learning goals, task completion status, roadmap preferences, and other learning-related information you enter into the App

We do not collect your name, phone number, precise location, or any payment information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

- **Providing learning roadmaps**: Your goals and preferences are used to generate personalized AI-powered roadmaps
- **Improving the App**: Aggregated usage data helps us understand how the App is used and identify areas for improvement
- **Notifications**: If you have provided an email, we may send service-related communications (e.g., feature updates or important notices). We do not send marketing emails.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

Pathcraft stores your progress and preference data **locally on your device** using Hive, a lightweight local storage solution.

Currently, the App does not use a cloud backend. Your data remains on your device unless you choose to share it (e.g., via device backups). Because data is stored locally, you are responsible for safeguarding your device and any backups.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data within the App, but no local storage method is completely secure. We encourage you to use device-level security features (e.g., passcodes, biometric locks).

## 4. Third-Party Services

Pathcraft uses the **Google Gemini API** to generate AI-powered learning roadmaps. When you request a roadmap, relevant information (such as your learning goals and preferences) is sent to Google's servers for processing. Google's use of this data is governed by the [Google Generative AI Prohibited Use Policy](https://policies.google.com/terms/generative-ai) and [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

No other third-party services have access to your data through the App.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: You can view all data stored by the App at any time through the App's interface
- **Deletion**: You can delete your data by clearing the App's local storage or uninstalling the App
- **Opt-out**: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App. Since data is stored locally, opting out means ceasing use entirely

Because we do not operate a cloud backend, we do not maintain a central database of user information. To exercise any rights, you can manage your data directly through the App or your device settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Pathcraft is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
